WORLD FIRST THROUGH-MAINBOARD WATERBLOCK,
NEW INNOVATION FOR PC WATERCOOLING INDUSTRY

For years, the CPU block design was stagnant with the same old recipe. Even with added screen, sensor or RGB lighting, the flow path was basically always go to the top side, often disrupting the harmony of carefully crafted setups. It require more creative to put into tubing path going across the front of most components.

Introducing the new original idea from Modding Cafe R&D: Stealth Tubing CPU block that route all water inlet and outlet to the backside through mainboard mounting holes. This is the result from our on-going development since 2020.

Along with mainboards using backside connectors to hide the wiring better, this new type of waterblock with greatly improve the clean aesthetic of future enthusiast's pc builds.

We currently already working with a few top-tier brands around the world to make this type of block accessible to everyone. If you are computer hardware manufacturer, you are welcome to contact us and we can all make them a new standard feature!

THROUGH-MAINBOARD SPECIAL FITTINGS
DEVELOPED BY MODDINGCAFE TEAM

Water inlets and outlets are hidden at the underside, coolant going through the block via special fittings, sealed using multiple silicone gasket o-rings inside.

We carefully tested to ensure meeting both requirement for flowrate and leak-free daily usage.

Clean look at the CPU waterblock, without tubing crossing over mainboard components. Everything is contained within CPU area from top down view. This type of waterblock pairing with mainboard using backside connectors like Asus TUF BTF Series (pictured), would elevate your pc case setup to the new level of cleanliness.
