REVOLUTIONIZING PC WATERCOOLING: INTRODUCING
THE WORLD'S FIRST THROUGH-MAINBOARD WATERBLOCK

For years, the PC cooling industry has been stuck in a repetitive cycle of CPU block designs. Despite adding screens, sensors, and RGB lighting, the traditional flow path always disrupted the sleek aesthetics of meticulously crafted setups. Huge amount of creativity was required to route tubing across the front of components, everytime.

Enter Modding Cafe R&D's groundbreaking innovation: The Stealth Tubing CPU block. This ingenious design routes all water inlet and outlet connections to the backside through the mainboard mounting holes.. The concept was in development since 2020, and this brand new type of CPU waterblock is set to transform the world of PC watercooling.

In synergy with the trend of mainboards using backside connectors to conceal wiring, our new waterblock promises to elevate the clean aesthetic of future PC enthusiast builds.

We're proud to announce that we are already collaborating with top-tier brands worldwide to make this cutting-edge waterblock accessible to everyone. If you're a computer hardware manufacturer, we invite you to reach out to us and join us in setting a new industry standard!

THROUGH-MAINBOARD SPECIAL FITTINGS
DEVELOPED BY MODDINGCAFE TEAM

Unlike traditional setups, our design conceals water inlets and outlets on the underside, allowing coolant to flow seamlessly through the block via unique fittings, all securely sealed with o-rings.

Our meticulous testing guarantees that these fittings not only meet the standard flowrate but also ensure leak-free, worry-free daily usage.

UNOBSTRUCTED VIEW

Enjoy an unobstructed view of the CPU waterblock, free from the clutter of tubing crossing over vital mainboard components. Everything is neatly contained within the CPU area, creating a pristine top-down view.

When combined with a mainboard featuring backside connectors, such as the Asus TUF BTF Series (as pictured), this type of waterblock will bring your PC case setup to the pinnacle of minimalistic aesthetics.
